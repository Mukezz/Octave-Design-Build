Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and process.

What is the typical project timeline? ‚ñº

Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. Residential projects typically take 8-18 months from design to completion. Commercial projects may take 12-24 months. We provide detailed timelines during the consultation phase and maintain regular communication throughout.

Do you handle the permitting process? ‚ñº

Yes, we manage the entire permitting process. Our team handles all municipal submissions, applications, and approvals. This ensures compliance with local building codes and regulations, streamlining the process for you.

What are your service areas? ‚ñº

We primarily serve British Columbia, focusing on the Greater Vancouver area and surrounding regions. For larger projects, we work with trusted partners across Canada. Please contact us to discuss your specific location and needs.

Do you offer sustainable building options? ‚ñº

Absolutely. Sustainability is core to our mission. We specialize in LEED certification, energy-efficient design, eco-friendly materials, and renewable energy integration. We can advise on sustainability options that fit your budget and goals.

What is included in a project consultation? ‚ñº

A consultation typically includes: site assessment, discussion of your vision and goals, budget review, preliminary timeline, service recommendations, and next steps. This is a no-obligation opportunity for us to understand your project and provide guidance.

How much does design work cost? ‚ñº

Design costs vary based on project complexity and size. Residential projects typically start at $15,000, while commercial projects start at $30,000. We'll provide a detailed proposal after understanding your specific requirements and budget.

Do you provide warranty on completed projects? ‚ñº

Yes. All construction work is covered by a comprehensive warranty. We provide a 1-year warranty on workmanship and 2-year warranties on major systems. We also arrange extended warranties on products and appliances where available.

Can you handle renovation projects? ‚ñº

Yes, we specialize in renovation and adaptive reuse projects. Whether it's a kitchen update, complete home modernization, or historic building restoration, our team has extensive experience managing complex renovation challenges.
