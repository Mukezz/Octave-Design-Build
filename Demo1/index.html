Modern Urban Residence

Project Overview

Contemporary 5,000 sq ft residence featuring floor-to-ceiling windows with panoramic city views. The design emphasizes open-concept living with high-end finishes throughout.

Key Features

  • Steel frame construction for maximum flexibility
  • Solar panel integration on rooftop
  • Smart home automation system
  • Sustainable hardwood flooring
  • Energy-efficient HVAC system

Project Timeline

Duration: 18 months

Completion: June 2023

Technologies Used

Steel Frame | Solar Integration | Smart Home Tech | Sustainable Materials

Challenges & Solutions

Challenge: Integrating solar panels without affecting architectural aesthetics.

Solution: Custom-designed integrated solar roof system that complements the modern design language.

Eco-Friendly Office Complex

Project Overview

LEED Certified commercial building designed for 250+ employees. Features green spaces, natural lighting, and sustainable materials throughout. The design prioritizes employee wellness and environmental responsibility.

Key Features

  • LEED Gold Certification
  • Green rooftop with native plants
  • Rainwater harvesting system
  • 92% recycled materials in construction
  • Advanced air quality management

Project Timeline

Duration: 24 months

Completion: March 2024

Technologies Used

Sustainable Materials | Green Roofing | Water Harvesting | Air Quality Systems

Impact

Reduces annual energy consumption by 40% compared to conventional office buildings. Supports the client's corporate sustainability goals and attracts environmentally conscious talent.

Historic Renovation & Modernization

Project Overview

Adaptive reuse project transforming a 1920s heritage building into luxury residential lofts. The renovation preserves the building's historic character while introducing modern amenities and contemporary design elements.

Key Features

  • Preserved original brick and timber facades
  • Modern steel reinforcement for structural integrity
  • High-tech systems in period-appropriate finishes
  • Heritage-approved restoration methods
  • 12-unit luxury loft conversion

Project Timeline

Duration: 20 months

Completion: November 2023

Technologies Used

Heritage Restoration | Structural Reinforcement | Adaptive Reuse | Modern Systems

Heritage Compliance

Project achieved Heritage Conservation Award. All modifications met strict heritage preservation guidelines while achieving modern living standards and energy efficiency.
