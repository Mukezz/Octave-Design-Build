Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical project take?

+

Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. A residential renovation typically takes 3-6 months, while a new build can take 12-18 months. Commercial projects may range from 6 months to 2+ years. We provide detailed timelines during the initial consultation.

Do you provide free consultations?

+

Yes! We offer free initial consultations to discuss your project, answer questions, and provide preliminary guidance. This helps us understand your needs and allows you to learn more about our services.

What areas do you serve?

+

We primarily serve the Greater Vancouver Area and surrounding regions in British Columbia. We also take on select projects in other major Canadian cities including Toronto, Calgary, and Montreal.

Do you work with existing architects or designers?

+

Absolutely! We collaborate with external architects, designers, and consultants. Our team is experienced in working with various professionals to ensure seamless project execution.

What is your payment structure?

+

Payment terms are customized based on project scope. Typically, we use milestone-based payments: initial deposit, progress payments at key stages, and final payment upon completion. All terms are clearly outlined in our contracts.

Are you licensed and insured?

+

Yes, we are fully licensed, bonded, and insured. We carry comprehensive liability insurance and workers' compensation coverage. All our contractors and subcontractors are also properly licensed and insured.
