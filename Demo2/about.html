About Octave

Building excellence since 2015

Our Story

Founded in 2015, Octave Design and Build emerged from a simple vision: to create spaces that harmonize form, function, and sustainability. What started as a small team of passionate architects and builders has grown into a trusted name in premium construction across Canada.

We believe that every project tells a story, and our role is to ensure that story is told beautifullyâ€”through thoughtful design, meticulous craftsmanship, and unwavering attention to detail. From residential estates to commercial complexes, we approach each project with the same commitment to excellence.

Our journey has been marked by innovation, from pioneering sustainable building practices to integrating cutting-edge technology in our design process. Today, we stand proud of over 50 completed projects, numerous industry awards, and most importantly, a 100% client satisfaction rate.
